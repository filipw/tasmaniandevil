1. Microsoft Presidio
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The analyzer recognizers (the generic and country-specific regex patterns,
checksum/validation logic, and context word lists), the lemma-based context-aware
score enhancement approach, and the anonymizer engine and operators in this package
are a C# port of, and adaptation of, Microsoft Presidio (presidio-analyzer and
presidio-anonymizer). The country recognizer packs ported here cover the United
States, United Kingdom, Germany, India, Italy and Spain.

3. Public-domain algorithms
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The context-aware enhancer uses the Porter stemming algorithm (M.F. Porter, 1980),
which is in the public domain. Several country recognizers use published, public
checksum algorithms (Luhn, ISO 7064 Mod 11,10, ICAO Doc 9303 Machine Readable
Travel Document check digit, the Verhoeff scheme, and various national mod-23 /
mod-26 control characters); these algorithms carry no licensing restriction.
